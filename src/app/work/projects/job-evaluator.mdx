## Overview

Make smarter career decisions using Job Evaluator. Using the Preference Selection Index (PSI) method to evaluate and rank job alternatives based on multiple criteria with scientific precision.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Criteria Analysis**: Evaluate 10 or more key of criterias including salary, location, career opportunities, and more.
- **Scientific Method**: Based on peer-reviewed PSI methodology with 4-decimal precision calculations.
- **Track and Compare**: Save evaluations, view history, comparing results and export reports in PDF, Excel, or CSV.
